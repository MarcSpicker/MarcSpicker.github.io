Marc Spicker

About me

I'm a postdoctoral researcher at the Visual Computing Group of Prof. Oliver Deussen at the University of Konstanz, Germany. My main research is done within the Collaborative Research Center SFB-TRR 161 where I work on quantitative models for visual abstraction. My research interests include computer graphics, especially the areas of real-time and non-photorealistic rendering, as well as information visualization. Contact information can be found on my institute website. Full CV upon request.
